Windy River Symphony Orchestra

The Beauty of Music...

Welcome to the Windy River Symphony Orchestra. Based in Northeastern Oregon, the WRSO is a fellowship of musicians committed to creating, providing and advocating for music in the communities around us. With a repertoire spanning from classical to pop, we aim to inspire musicians of all ages.

Come join our ranks today!
