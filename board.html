Windy River Symphony Orchestra

Board Members

Scrooge McDuck is the sole proprietor of over 500 international properties and businesses. From an early age, he has saved his money with the single goal of providing for his family. He has appeared in numerous films, including Mickey's Christmas Carol and DuckTales the Movie: Treasure of the Lost Lamp.
From a young age, Moana has loved the ocean. As a young woman, she would often venture across the ocean in search of food and other staples for her island community of Motunui. Ever faithful to her family and adventuring spirit, Moana adds her years of leadership experience to the Windy River Symphony Orchestra.
Orphaned in a sideshow circus at the tender age of 18 weeks, Erik, more commonly known by his alias "The Phantom of the Opera" (Which opera, we're still trying to figure out) has overcome some stiff odds. And yet, Erik brings to Windy River Symphony Orchestra his undeniable taste in music and love for money. A self-proclaimed "ladies man" he runs his own music studio that has output some of France's finest female stars. Since moving to Umatilla, he has opened his studio to high school youth. Contact him today for voice lessons. He'll help you give your LIFE to music!
