Windy River Symphony Orchestra

About Us

Based in Northeastern Oregon in the city of Umatilla, the Windy River Symphony Orchestra is an all-volunteer organization committed to enriching the lives and communities around us through music.

Led by Music Director Ashley Walchli, the WRSO aims to be a home for musicians of all ages and abilities to share and develop their love of music.

Are you a musician who would like to play with us? We would love to have you join our ranks! All rehearsals take place at Umatilla High School on Mondays at 6pm. Please contact us for more information.

Donate to WRSO Online!

The Windy River Symphony Orchestra is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization. We are sustained by generous donations from people like you. Please consider donating to WRSO and help us enrich lives through music!
